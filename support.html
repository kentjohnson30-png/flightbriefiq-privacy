Getting Started

FlightBriefIQ includes a comprehensive in-app user manual and interactive tutorial. You can access these from the Settings tab at any time.

Common Questions

How do I set up my aircraft?

Go to Settings > My Aircraft. You can create up to 5 aircraft profiles with your specific performance data, limitations, and equipment.

How does fatigue tracking work?

FlightBriefIQ can import your sleep history from Apple Health, or you can enter it manually using the sleep timeline editor on the Fatigue tab. The app uses your sleep data to predict fatigue levels at each phase of your flight.

Where does the weather data come from?

Weather data (METARs, TAFs, AIRMETs, SIGMETs, winds aloft, and PIREPs) is sourced from the FAA Aviation Weather Center. NOTAMs are retrieved from the FAA NOTAM API.

Is my data private?

Yes. All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is transmitted to any server. See our Privacy Policy for details.
